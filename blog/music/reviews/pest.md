This is a band I wished I discovered earlier. While this is definitely "girl music" I think this is pretty darkly clever, plenty of curse words, and epic highs and lows. Do I relate to this person? Not really. But the music is insanely good. If you listen to any of it, listen to "Poor Madeline".
