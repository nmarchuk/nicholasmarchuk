I think there is a sense that Snail Mail needs to be graded on the curve since she had vocal surgery. I would argue that she clearly is still a great singer, she just needs better ideas and way less production. There is quite enough pop out there - she can and should make sentimental indie rock, or at least indie dream pop, like her previous albums. If she doesn't want to be a singer anymore, that's fine, and I think nobody should be forced to make music. I think what people don't realize is that there's a lot of people who do not want music by people that "can sing", that are trained and have impeccable production. That doesn't read as honest anymore.
