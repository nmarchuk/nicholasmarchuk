Nicholas Marchuk designs heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems for laboratories and offices in Greater Boston.

He also is an independent author of heartfelt and entertaining fiction beloved by regular people with good taste.

He is a fixture at music venues, front porches, and hockey rinks, where he plays trombone with the same joy he imbues in the written word.

His books record the kindness and absurdity of modern life as his characters struggle to find their place within it.
