website

This website is a static HTML, CSS, and JS website built with Eleventy (11ty). I programmed, wrote, and deployed it myself. I think programming is a fun skill that anyone can and should learn. It has never been easier to build an 'old school' style website.

The font is JetBrains Mono. It's a font I love because it is the font for IntelliJ, the Java IDE. It is also free and open source.

life

For writing, the most formative experience in my life was what I experienced in high school. I was fortunate enough to take a self-directed creative writing course with a teacher by the name of Ms. Hocking, who gave me the confidence and tools to pursue this at a deeper level. There were a lot of other teachers at all levels of education that I also owe a debt to, mostly for their kindness and generosity with their time as I grew up.

I also owe a debt of gratitude to my parents and my friends. I hope that they all know how much they mean to me.
